SDBOX is an additional software (Add-on) to CAD platforms and provides tools that help the processing and management of any design as well as the drafting of complete studies.

For the last 10 years our main concern, as a software development company, is that when our customers buy our software they use it in the best possible way and support.

Basic principals for creating sdbox:

  • The evolution of the successful and popular greek version to reach more users.
  • The automation of frequently repeated design processes.
  • The user-selected Template-Based Design organization.
  • The provision of appropriate tools for symbols, texts, numbers, dimensions, etc.
  • The easy creation and management of libraries.

CONTENTS

Math operations and numbers

Automatic calculations/recording of surfaces and lengths, operations with and on objects, automatic dimensioning, deviation calculation, slopes, etc.

Compilation of studies

Topographic-Survey Plans, Floor Plans-Elevation Plans-Section Plans, Building Plans, Fire Protection, Land Registry Plans, Site Support Plans, Photo Issue etc

Design automations

Counts, measurements, automatic memos, tables, mass object changes, etc.

Design management

Mass conversions / corrections / additions / replacements / export-import of texts and symbols, management-adjustment of images, line conversions, layer management, etc.

Files and Folders

File import / export, file editor, automatic opening of folders, etc.

Transfer design to Google Earth

Select objects (ines, plines, splines, arcs, circles, ellipses, points, blocks, texts, images etc) from the AutoCAD screen and automatically display them to GE preserving the original layers.

Print

Automatic creation of pdf and dxf, export to a new design, preset ctb's for each scale, etc.
